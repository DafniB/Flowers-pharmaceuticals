About Us

Flowers Pharmaceuticals is the largest contract manufacturing nutraceuticals company of India. The organization deals in the manufacturing and export of formulations of Nutraceutical segments. The company is currently supplying to almost all India pharmaceutical companies across the globe. The state of-the-art facilities are dedicated to Ayurvedic food supplements & nutraceuticals and animal health care. Flowers Pharmaceuticals has been successful in attaining national and international accreditations and building trust on Efficacy, Safety & Quality. The organization is certified with WHO-GMP, ISO 9001:2015, FSSAI and ISO 22000:2018 certificates.
